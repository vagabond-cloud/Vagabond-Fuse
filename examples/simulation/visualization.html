EU Digital ID Wallet Simulation

Cross-border identity verification using XRPL and Verifiable Credentials

This simulation demonstrates how the European Digital Identity Wallet (EUDI) can be used to access public services across EU member states, following the scenario depicted in the provided image. The simulation uses the Vagabond-Fuse framework with XRPL integration for DIDs and verifiable credentials.

1

Alma opens her EU Digital Identity Wallet

Alma, a 29-year-old Spanish citizen living in Portugal, opens her EU Digital Identity Wallet app on her smartphone. The wallet connects to the XRPL testnet and retrieves her digital identity.

const almaWallet = new EUDIWallet({
  userName: "Alma",
  userCountry: "Spain",
  networkUrl: XRPL_TESTNET
});

await almaWallet.connect();
2

Creating Alma's digital identity

The wallet creates a Decentralized Identifier (DID) for Alma on the XRPL. This DID serves as her unique digital identity that can be verified across different services and countries.

const almaAddress = almaWallet.getAddress();
const almaDID = new XrplDID(almaAddress);
console.log(`Alma's DID: ${almaDID.getDID()}`);
3

Spanish authority issues identification credential

The Spanish identification authority issues a verifiable credential to Alma's wallet. This credential contains her identification information in a secure, tamper-evident format.

const spanishAuthority = new SpanishIdentificationAuthority();

const identificationCredential = spanishAuthority.issueIdentificationCredential(
  almaDID.getDID(),
  {
    name: "Alma",
    dateOfBirth: "1994-05-15",
    nationality: "Spanish",
    documentNumber: "ES123456789"
  }
);
4

Alma opens the Portuguese National Population Register app

Alma opens the Portuguese National Population Register app on her phone. This app allows residents to update their address information in the national registry.

const portugueseRegister = new PopulationRegisterService("PT");
5

Alma authenticates with her Spanish identification

The Portuguese app requests identity verification. Alma's EUDI wallet presents her Spanish identification credential, which is verified by the Portuguese service without requiring additional documentation.

const verified = await portugueseRegister.verifyIdentity(
  almaDID.getDID(),
  identificationCredential
);
6

Alma updates her address in the Portuguese National Population Register

After successful authentication, Alma submits her new address details. The Portuguese National Population Register updates her information in their system.

const addressUpdated = await portugueseRegister.updateAddress(
  almaDID.getDID(),
  {
    street: "Rua da Liberdade 123",
    city: "Lisbon",
    postalCode: "1250-096",
    country: "Portugal"
  }
);

Benefits of EU Digital ID Wallet

Seamless Cross-Border Services

Access public services across EU member states without bureaucratic hurdles.

Secure Identity Verification

Cryptographically secure verification without revealing unnecessary personal data.

User Control

Citizens maintain control over their identity data and choose what to share.

Paperless Procedures

Reduces the need for physical documents and in-person visits to government offices.
